# DreamLoop

DreamLoop is a shared AI-generated adventure experience designed for two users.

It enables long-distance couples or friends to participate in a continuously evolving story world.

Users receive AI-generated story events and must choose between multiple options. The story progresses based on their combined decisions.

The product focuses on creating shared experiences asynchronously.

---

## Core Concept

Two players are placed inside an infinite AI-generated story world.

Each event presents a situation and three choices.

Both players make decisions.

Once both decisions are submitted, the story progresses to the next event.

---

## Target Users

- Long-distance couples
- Long-distance friends
- People looking for shared experiences

---

## Emotional Tone

The app should feel:

- cute
- cozy
- adventurous
- occasionally mysterious or spooky

---

## Product Experience

The app acts as a shared narrative game.

Players explore a story together through decision making.

Each decision influences the direction of the story.

The experience should feel like a collaborative adventure.

---

## MVP Scope

The MVP must support:

- authentication
- partner invite system
- AI-generated story events
- decision selection
- story progression
- real-time sync between users
