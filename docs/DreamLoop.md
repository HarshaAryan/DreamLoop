# DreamLoop
### Shared AI Dream Adventure for Long-Distance Relationships

A home screen widget-based shared pixel world where couples or friends experience AI-generated adventures together.

---

# 1. Vision

DreamLoop is a shared digital world that lives on your phone’s home screen.

Two people share a tiny pixel universe where:
- adventures unfold
- weather changes
- characters move
- decisions shape the story

The goal is to create a **daily ritual of shared curiosity and connection**.

Users should feel:

> "I wonder what happened in our world today."

DreamLoop is designed to feel:
- cute
- cozy
- adventurous
- occasionally mysterious or scary

The emotional tone should feel like:
- a bedtime story
- a cozy pixel game
- a shared dream

---

# 2. Problem Statement

## Long Distance Relationship Problems

People in long-distance relationships experience:

### 1. Boredom in communication
Most interactions become repetitive:
- texting
- video calls
- watching movies

Conversations become routine.

### 2. Lack of shared experiences
Couples or friends cannot:
- explore together
- go on adventures
- create memories

### 3. Time-zone differences
Users are rarely available simultaneously.

### 4. Emotional distance
Relationships weaken when interactions become purely conversational.

### 5. No shared ritual
Successful digital products create habits.

Most communication apps do not create daily curiosity.

---

# 3. Product Idea

DreamLoop creates a **shared evolving pixel world** that both users influence.

The world lives as a **home screen widget**.

Users interact with the world through:
- story choices
- events
- exploration
- emotional moments

The world evolves based on their decisions.

---

# 4. Core Experience

## Shared Pixel Characters

Each user has a character.

Characters live in the shared world.

Characters:
- walk
- sit
- explore
- react to events

Visual style:
- pixel art
- cute
- expressive

---

## Shared World

The world evolves over time.

Possible worlds include:

- enchanted forest
- floating islands
- space station
- cozy village
- underwater kingdom
- haunted mansion
- dream landscapes

World changes based on story progress.

---

## Story Events

The system generates story events.

Example:

> A glowing cave appears near your village.

Options appear for both users:

1. Enter the cave
2. Camp outside
3. Send a drone inside

Both users choose independently.

Once both choose, the story progresses.

---

## Scene Generation

After a decision:

A new scene appears showing what happened.

Example:

> You enter the cave and discover glowing crystals.

The widget updates visually.
